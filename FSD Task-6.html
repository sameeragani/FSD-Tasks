🌈 The World of Colours 🌈

Introduction

Colours make our world beautiful and vibrant. They influence our emotions, creativity, and the way we perceive our surroundings. From nature to art, colours play an important role in everyday life.

Importance of Colours

Different colours create different feelings. Blue often represents calmness, green symbolizes nature, red expresses energy, and yellow brings happiness. Colours are widely used in design, fashion, and communication.

Colours add beauty, meaning, and emotion to our lives.
